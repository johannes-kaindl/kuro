## Why AGPL-3.0 for the code

The previous public lineage of this theme carried an MIT notice. The relicense to
AGPL-3.0 is made by **Jay (v6t2b9)**, the sole author of the code, which makes the
change permissible. AGPL-3.0 is explicitly accepted for the Obsidian community
catalogue (precedent: Templater).

## Why CC BY-SA 4.0 for the docs

Prose and documentation are not well served by a software license. CC BY-SA 4.0 keeps
the documentation free and share-alike while staying compatible with reuse in wikis and
derivative guides.

## Fonts

The four embedded families (JetBrains Mono, Space Grotesk, Inter, EB Garamond) are each
licensed under the SIL Open Font License 1.1 and are redistributed here as Latin subsets.
The OFL permits embedding and redistribution; the fonts retain their own license and are
not relicensed under AGPL.
